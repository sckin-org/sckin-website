Privacy policy

How SCKIN collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: 22 July 2026

What we collect

SCKIN collects only what is needed to operate SickleCellPedia and keep you informed when you ask us to. Questions you ask SickleCellPedia are processed to generate an answer and are not linked to your identity. See our Responsible AI commitments for how model inputs are handled.

Information you provide

  • Email address, when you subscribe to the newsletter
  • Name, role, and location, when you register interest in SickleCellPedia Pro
  • Payment details, processed by Stripe — never stored by SCKIN

Information we never collect

We do not collect medical records, and we do not sell or share personal information with advertisers — full stop.

Our mission

Our mission is to make useful and reliable information about sickle cell disease universally accessible.

Sickle cell disease is the world's most common inherited blood disorder — and the knowledge to manage it is hardest to find where it is needed most. SCKIN closes that gap with accurate, multilingual tools, free for everyone.

Free, always
No paywalls, no accounts required.
Multilingual by default
Built for the languages patients actually speak.
Evidence-based
Grounded in current clinical guidance.
